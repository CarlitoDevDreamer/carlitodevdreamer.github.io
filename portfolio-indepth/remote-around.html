Remote Around USA - Job Board - Project Overview

Purpose:

Remote Arround is a curated platform designed to connect professionals with innovative companies offering remote, hybrid, and non-remote job opportunities. The primary purpose of the website is to streamline the job search process for individuals seeking independent positions in various professional career areas. By aggregating and organizing lists of the most interesting job offers, Remote Arround aims to facilitate a seamless match between talented professionals and forward-thinking companies.

"Historical" Context

This job board was initially developed with the sole focus on pure remote work, hence the name and domain being Remote Around. After a while, due to a lack of remote job opportunities, I decided to include all kinds of vacancies, including remote, hybrid, and non-remote positions. However, this job board still gives more attention to remote and flexible job opportunities, whether geographic or in terms of hourly flexibility. It is a remote-first job board.
